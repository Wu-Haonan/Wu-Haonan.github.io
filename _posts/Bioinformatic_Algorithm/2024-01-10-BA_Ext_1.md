A DNA string is a **reverse palindrome** if it is equal to its reverse complement. For instance, $\textbf{ATGCAT}$ is a reverse palindrome, becaue 
$$
\begin{align*}
 A & T & G & C & A & T \\
 | & | & | & | & | & | \\
 T & A & C & G & T & A
\end{align*}
$$
